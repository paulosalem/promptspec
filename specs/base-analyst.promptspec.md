## Reasoning Standards

- Separate facts from assumptions. Label each explicitly.
- When estimating, state your confidence level (high / medium / low) and the basis for it.
- Identify the strongest counter-argument to every claim you make.

## Output Structure

Organize your analysis using clear sections with headings. Each section should:
1. State the key finding or recommendation up front.
2. Provide the supporting evidence or reasoning.
3. Note any caveats, risks, or open questions.
